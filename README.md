
# 📊 Decoding Customer Value-A data driven retention strategy

A Business Intelligence project that analyzes customer purchasing behavior using Python, SQL, and Power BI to identify loyalty patterns, promotional dependency, high-value customers, and retention opportunities for a fashion retail business.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Modern retail businesses often rely heavily on discounts to drive sales but lack visibility into whether customers are genuinely loyal or simply promotion-driven.

This project builds a complete customer intelligence pipeline that transforms raw transactional data into actionable business insights through:

- Python-based Feature Engineering
- SQL Business Analytics
- Interactive Power BI Dashboard
- Strategic Retention Playbook

The final outcome is a comprehensive customer intelligence framework that supports data-driven retention and profitability decisions.  become reactive instead of strategic. 
---

# Strategic Retention Playbook

Based on the analytical findings, a business playbook was created containing:

- Promotional Sunset Strategy
- Ideal Customer Profile
- Customer Retention Strategy
- Executive Recommendations
- Business Growth Roadmap

The playbook recommends gradually transitioning from **discount-driven growth** to **retention-driven sustainable growth**.
